About Me

I'm a Front-End Developer, UX Engineer and UX Designer with over 12 years of professional experience. I specialize in designing and building responsive websites, creating prototypes and implementing web analytics. I'm also interested in AI Art and have included some recent pieces in this portfolio.

Having a Bachelors degree in Graphic Design and years of shadowing some of the best UX Designers has taught me to design and code from a place of empathy for both clients and users. In addition, I'm detail-oriented and a problem solver. My motto is "if there's a way, I'll find it".

When I'm not working, you'll find me either spending time with my son Conrad, learning about or discussing the benefits of good nutrition, singing or listening to music and/or discussing various topics such as spirituality, relationships and everything in between.
