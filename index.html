About Me

I'm a Design Technologist, Front-End Developer, and UX Engineer with over 15 years of experience in designing and building responsive websites, developing interactive user experiences, and optimizing web performance. I specialize in translating ideas into functional, user-friendly digital products, with a strong focus on accessibility, usability, and scalable front-end architecture.

With a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design and years of collaborating with top UX Designers, I've learned to approach design and development with empathy, ensuring both client and user needs are met effectively. Throughout my career, I've led front-end development for government and private-sector projects, building solutions from the ground up, refining user flows, and solving complex UI challenges. I thrive on problem-solving and continuous learning, always seeking new ways to improve and innovate. My motto? "If there's a way, I'll find it."

Beyond my work, I create both traditional and AI art, combining creativity and technology to push artistic boundaries. When I'm not coding or designing, you'll find me spending time with my son Conrad, diving into discussions about nutrition, spirituality, relationships, and everything in between, or simply enjoying music and singing.
